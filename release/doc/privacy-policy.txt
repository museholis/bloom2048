🌸 Bloom 2048 Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 12, 2026

================================================================

1. Information We Collect

Bloom 2048 ("the App") does not collect any personal information.

- No account registration is required.
- No personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number) is collected.
- No location data is collected.

================================================================

2. Data Stored on Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

- Game high scores
- Sound and vibration settings

This data is never transmitted outside the device and is deleted when the App is uninstalled.

================================================================

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services such as advertising, analytics, or social media integrations.

================================================================

4. Children's Privacy

The App does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13. Since the App does not collect any personal data, it is safe for users of all ages.

================================================================

5. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Changes will be communicated through app updates.
